Overview

BayaniPay is an online remittance platform for money sent from the US to the Philippines. Is is a partnership among Talino Venture Labs, The Asian Journal, Wavemaker Partners, and is the authorized agent of BDO Unibank., the Philippines' largest bank.

To date, BayaniPay has processed over $8 million USD remittances since its launch last February 2022.
